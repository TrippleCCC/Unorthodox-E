About Us

Unorthodox-E is comprised of 4 team members, all college freshman across the state of Texas: Didi, Nonso, Shreya, and Juan. The group was formed at Google CSSI (Computer Science Summer Institute).

About Unorthodox-Eats

Unorthodox-Eats aims to bring awareness to the issue of food insufficiency, especially in the state of Texas. The project was cultivated in July 2018 and has been growing since.

About Food Insufficiency in Texas

The Stats

1 in 4 children and 1 in 7 adults struggle with food insufficiency in Texas.
